Refund Policy

Egg Smash Adventures — Last updated: April 2026

All purchases in Egg Smash Adventures are for virtual in-game items that are delivered instantly upon payment. Please read this policy carefully before making a purchase.

No Refunds on Delivered Items

Because virtual items (gold coins, tool packs, and permanent upgrades) are delivered to your account immediately upon successful payment, all sales are final and non-refundable under normal circumstances.

Exceptions

We will issue a refund or credit in the following cases:

  • Payment was successfully charged but the items were never delivered to your account due to a technical error
  • A duplicate charge occurred for the same purchase
  • You are entitled to a refund under applicable consumer protection law in your country

How to Request a Refund

If you believe you qualify for a refund, contact us within 14 days of your purchase at yotam@exacti.us with:

  • Your device ID or account details
  • The item purchased and the date of purchase
  • A brief description of the issue

We aim to respond within 2 business days.

Contact

For any questions about this policy, email us at yotam@exacti.us.
