Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: April 2026

Fan Tribute Disclaimer

Egg Breaker Adventure Revival is an independent fan tribute inspired by the original Egg Breaker game (2008–2016). It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, sponsored by, or connected in any way to the original developers or rights holders. All code, artwork, and game design in this version are original work created by the fan developer. Attempts have been made to contact the original creators to ensure this tribute is respectful of their work.

Overview

Egg Breaker Adventure Revival is a free browser-based game available on web and Android. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and what third-party services are involved.

Game Progress (Local Storage)

Your game progress — gold, collection items, achievements, and settings — is saved on your device using your browser's localStorage. This data stays on your device unless you choose to enable Cloud Save.

Cloud Save (Optional)

If you choose to enable Cloud Save, you will be asked to sign in with a Google account. This uses Google OAuth via Supabase, a third-party backend service. Your game save data is stored securely on Supabase servers and is associated with your Google account. You can unlink your account or delete your cloud save at any time from within the game settings. Cloud Save is entirely optional.

Payments

Optional in-app purchases are processed through PayPal. No payment card details are collected or stored by this app. Purchase records (product ID, amount, status) are stored on Supabase servers to verify and restore purchases. This app does not store your PayPal credentials.

Analytics

This app uses Umami, a privacy-focused analytics service. Umami does not use cookies, does not track users across sites, and does not collect personally identifiable information. It records anonymous events such as game actions to help improve the game.

Error Tracking

This app uses GlitchTip for crash and error reporting. When an error occurs, basic technical information (error message, stack trace, browser type, app version) is sent to GlitchTip servers. No personal data is included in these reports.

Service Worker & Offline Caching

The game installs a Service Worker to allow offline play. This caches only the game's own assets (HTML, CSS, JS, images) on your device. No user data is included in this cache.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The optional Cloud Save and payment features require a Google account or PayPal account, which have their own age requirements.

Data Deletion

To delete your local save data, use Settings → Reset All Progress within the game. To delete your cloud save, unlink your Google account from within Settings → Cloud Save. To request deletion of purchase records, contact us at the address below.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
