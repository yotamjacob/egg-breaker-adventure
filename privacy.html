🥚 Egg Breaker Adventures

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Egg Breaker Adventures is a free browser-based game. We take privacy seriously and have designed the game to collect as little data as possible.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration is required.
  • No name, email address, or contact information is collected.
  • No location data is collected.
  • No advertising identifiers are used.
  • No analytics or tracking scripts are embedded.

Local Storage

Your game progress (gold, collection items, achievements, settings) is saved entirely on your own device using your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to any server.

You can export, import, or delete your save at any time via the Settings → Save / Load menu within the game.

Service Worker & Offline Caching

The game installs a Service Worker to allow offline play. This caches only the game's own assets (HTML, CSS, JS, images) on your device. No user data is included in this cache.

Third-Party Services

The game loads the Press Start 2P font from Google Fonts. This is a standard web font request and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. No other third-party services are used.

Children's Privacy

Egg Breaker Adventures does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Because no personal data is collected from any user, the game is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to the developer via the game's support page or the app store listing.
